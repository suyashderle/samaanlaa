Furniture Materials

  • Plywood – Strong base material used for making tables, cupboards, and beds.
  • MDF (Medium Density Fibreboard) – Smooth surface, good for painted furniture and decorative panels.
  • Laminates – Protective sheets available in different colours and textures for finishing.
  • Veneer – Thin natural wood layer used for premium look.
  • Hardware (Hinges, Handles, Screws) – Essential fittings for assembling furniture.
  • Foam & Upholstery – Soft materials for sofa, chairs, and cushions.
  • Polish & Paint – For finishing and protecting wood surfaces.
